About MedSys

Reagent Inventory System (REGIS)

MedSys

Version 1.0.0 · Reagent Inventory System (REGIS)

A purpose-built digital solution for hospital and clinical laboratory professionals to manage reagent inventory, track expiry dates, monitor stock levels, and generate compliance-ready reports.

ISO 15189 Aligned QR + Barcode Scanning Mobile Ready Digital Reports

QR Code & Barcode Scanning

Identify reagents instantly by scanning QR codes or standard barcodes (EAN-13, Code 128, UPC) with any device camera.

Automated Alerts

Real-time dashboard alerts for low stock, critical levels, and approaching expiry dates with push notifications.

Digital Reports

Generate inventory, restocking, and usage reports — exportable as CSV for audit and compliance records.

Role-Based Access

Secure login with full audit trail for all inventory changes, usage logs, and report generation.
